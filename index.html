A smarter home for a happier you.

Meet the homeowner's digital assistant.

Haven is the future of homeownership, offering the information you need to keep your home operating at its best. So you can spend less time on your home and more with those inside it.

Never miss scheduled maintenance.

With maintenance reminders, you’ll know precisely when to change your air filter, what kind of filter to use and where to order it online. Scheduled maintenance lowers costs and extends appliance lifecycles.

Repair appliances before they break.

Haven predicts when your appliances will fail, helping you avoid down time when you need them most. When chances of failure are high, you’ll be connected with a trusted maintenance professional to make preventative repairs. Should your appliance require an upgrade, you’ll be given a list of replacements that best suit your needs, along with certified dealers in your area.
